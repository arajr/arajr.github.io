About

I build the platforms that investment teams depend on every day — and the engineering organizations that can keep building them reliably.

For the past 12 years at Wellington Management, I have served as principal architect and platform strategist at the center of enterprise investment technology. My work spans the full spectrum: owning cross-asset platform architecture, leading engineering teams, defining multi-year technology roadmaps with the CIO's office, and pioneering the firm's adoption of Generative AI and agentic engineering workflows.

Before Wellington, I spent 12 years at Sapient Corporation delivering complex systems across financial services and energy — trading gateways, risk platforms, regulatory reporting, and market data systems for clients including Royal Bank of Scotland, Duke Energy, and NY-ISO.

My current focus is the intersection of investment technology and AI: RAG pipelines, LLM evaluation frameworks, and agentic coding tools that measurably accelerate how engineering teams deliver. I bring hands-on technical depth most technology executives don't carry, and executive scope most engineers never develop.

Precise, outcome-oriented, and direct. I build clarity where there is complexity — and I will push for the right answer even when it is inconvenient. In investment technology, the cost of getting it wrong is never abstract.
